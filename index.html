JOIN US AS WE REBUILD THIS BEAUTIFUL AFRICA

Gifts From God Children's Ministry is a non-government Christian organisation in Uganda that focuses on helping the most vulnerable children in society and the poverty stricken community to benefit the immediate area and the country as a whole.

Vision

A HOLISTIC AND CHRISTIAN APPROACH TO CHILD DEVELOPMENT AND CARE.

Our work is centered around children. Their development comes first in every aspect of our work.

OUR MISSION AND VISION

Mission

Gifts from God Children's Ministry is in the business of transforming 'nobodies' into somebodies by giving them a family that cares and by putting effort into their spiritual well being, education and health. Our aim is to turn those in our care into confident people who are 'somebody'.
