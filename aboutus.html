How we started

In 2021 Gifts from God Ministry Uganda celebrarated its 2nd birthday.

Founded by Miss Sarah as a non-government Christian organization originally as a home for abandoned, abused and unwanted children, it has since expanded and its remit now supports Uganda's most vulnerable children and the poverty stricken community in the immediate vicinity of the compound.

The Future

Our mission and our work will not stop. Day after day we are working for a better future for our children and for Uganda. We will continue to provide children in need with safety, love and a home if necessary. We hope our children will one day start healthy families of their own and break the cycle of poverty, carelessness and abuse.

We welcome you to become a part of our work. Find out how you can Help.

Vision

A HOLISTIC AND CHRISTIAN APPROACH TO CHILD DEVELOPMENT AND CARE.

Our work is centered around children. Their development comes first in every aspect of our work.

OUR MISSION AND VISION

Mission

Gifts for All is in the business of transforming 'nobodies' into somebodies by giving them a family that cares and by putting effort into their spiritual well being, education and health. Our aim is to turn those in our care into confident people who are 'somebody'.
